Forecast Activity of Heavy Rainfall Precursors

Use the dropdown menus to monitor heavy rainfall precursor activity in the first 15 days of the ECMWF subseasonal ensemble forecast.

This webpage provides near-real-time monitoring of heavy rainfall precursors -- flow patterns which typically increase the risk of 90th percentile+ rainfall events in different regions of Europe and the Mediterranean. Precursor indices are standardised, with large positive values (heuristically; >1.5) indicating a substantially higher risk of heavy rainfall.

For more detail on the precursor approach, see our papers on this topic.

This is a research product provided with no guarantees of reliability: it should not be used in lieu of official forecasts from operational weather centres. Regions are defined based on a clustering algorithm which groups together locations with highly correlated daily rainfall. Names of regions are necessarily approximate, and should not be taken as an implied comment on geopolitical borders.
